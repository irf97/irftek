# Mesh Resource Ledger — the future's bill of materials
*IrfTek research frame · Jul 2026 · pairs with irftek app (mesh badges) · not investment advice*

## Premise
The corpus metric, applied to markets: **output ≠ money made; output = reduction in the resource
cost for civilization to run coordination.** The Living Mesh needs compute, energy, memory,
perception, a coordination fabric, actuation, and silicon manufacturing. The Reaching Economy
prices delegation; delegation gets cheap only when those seven inputs get cheap. Miras' rule
applies architecturally: this ledger allocates **custody-goods only** — trust, reputation,
meaning (standing-goods) are forbidden from distribution here by the corpus's own law, so no
ticker gets credit for "brand" or "community."

Each name gets: **mesh %** = its share of the achievable resource-cost reduction, and a
**c/o flag** = cost-to-output leverage (★ = small company, disproportionate lever: a $2B firm
removing 3% of AI power loss outleverages a $3T firm adding capacity).

## How this plugs into the app (guardrail)
mesh% is a **conviction/sizing tilt inside the R7 core sleeve** and a reason a name is in the
universe at all. It is **never a timing signal**: entries still require the gate (GO) or your
explicit TARGET call. A 9%-mesh name with STAND-DOWN is a 0%-position today. Vision ranks;
the gate times; you size.

## Honest bounds
These weights are structured judgments derived from the corpus (Living Mesh / Reaching
Economy / Miras), not measured flows — a prioritization instrument, revisited quarterly or
when an axis's physics changes (e.g., a Rubin-class efficiency jump re-prices the energy axis).
